Inspired by JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET exam patterns, these problems are tailored for exam preparation, offering a mix of numerical, conceptual, and derivation-based challenges. NEET-style problems (66â€“100) are formatted as multiple-choice questions (MCQs) to match the examâ€™s objective format. Problems are organized by type to support progressive learning and build confidence in mastering wave mechanics, a key topic for JEE/NEET success.
